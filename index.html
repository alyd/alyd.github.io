Hi, I am Alyssa Li Dayan (pronounced “A-lee-sa Lee Day-an”); I also go by Li.

I am a Computer Science Ph.D. student at Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research where I am advised by Stuart Russell. Before that, I was an ML research scientist at Uber ATG. I completed my BS in Math with CS at MIT where I did research with the Flavell, Cocosci and MIT-IBM Watson AI labs.

My research is supported by the Hertz Fellowship.
